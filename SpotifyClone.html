Languages Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

A web based Music Player inspired largely by Spotify. It plays songs saved in the project folder. It includes a playslist, forward, backward buttons, play/pause functionality.
Also has a seek bar indicating progress of the current song and can also be used to seek a particular point in the song.

Image of the webpage in full desktop mode.
A navigation bar can be seen on top with the Spotify logo and Home and about links. Center container has a playlist of songs. There is also a bottom area consisting of a seek bar, play, previous, next buttons and info about the current song being played.
